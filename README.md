Customer Churn Prediction and Customer Segmentation

Worked on a telecom customer dataset to understand why customers leave a service and how businesses can improve customer retention. Cleaned and analyzed customer data, explored patterns in customer behavior, and grouped customers into different segments based on their usage and spending habits.

Built and compared multiple machine learning models to predict customers who are most likely to churn. Identified key factors such as contract type, tenure, and monthly charges that influence customer decisions. Created visualizations and generated practical recommendations that could help a telecom company reduce customer attrition and improve customer loyalty.

Technologies: Python, Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-Learn, Matplotlib, Seaborn

Highlights:

Analyzed over 7,000 customer records.
Compared different machine learning models for churn prediction.
Segmented customers into loyal, regular, and high-risk groups.
Identified major reasons behind customer churn and suggested retention strategies.
